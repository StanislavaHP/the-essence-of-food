Chickpeas

The chickpea is one of the earliest cultivated legumes. It traces back to the Middle East. Rich in iron, it is the main ingredient in hummus.

Did you know?

Fun facts about the Chickpeas

Chickpeas are used to make flour.
The liquid from cans of chickpeas can be used as an egg white replacement in meringue and ice cream production.
In Germany chickpeas are known to have been brewed instead of coffee.
India produces more than half of the world's chickpeas.

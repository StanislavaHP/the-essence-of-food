Apple

The apple is a fruit which originated in Central Asia. Rich in vitamin C, it is a great snack, addition to smoothies and base for pies.

Fun facts about the Apple

Brought to North Amertica by European colonists in the 17th century.

Of the total world production of apples, China produces around 50%.

Toffee apples are popular confection made in the UK.

The proverb "An apple a day keeps the doctor away" traced to 19th-century Wales, used to be known as "an apple a day, no doctor to pay".

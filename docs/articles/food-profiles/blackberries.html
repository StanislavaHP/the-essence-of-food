Blackberries

The blackberry is a fruit which is native to Europe, Asia and the Americas. Rich in vitamins, it is a popular ingredient in jams and pies.

Did you know?

Fun facts about the Blackberries

Blackberries have been used as a dye for fabrics and hair.

There is an expression - "blackberries are red when they're green" because the fruit is red before it ripens.

Blackberries can be used to produce wine.

Mexico is the leading producer of blackberries.

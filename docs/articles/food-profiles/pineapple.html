Pineapple

The pineapple is a fruit. It originated in South America. Rich in vitamin C, the pineapple juice is a popular drink.

Did you know?

Fun facts about the Pineapple

Pollinated primarily by hummingbirds.
In architecture, the pineapple figure symbolises hospitality.
Costa Rica, Brazil and the Philippines are the leading producers of pineapple.
Pineapple juice is the main ingredient in Pina Colada.

How To Chop An Onion?

The Basics

Step by Step Guide (for finely chopped onion)

Step 1 Chop off the head and tail of the onion.

Step 2 You should now be able to easily remove the skin.

Step 3 Cut the onion in half.

Step 4 Place the onion half face down.

Step 5 Chop either along the x or y axis, then switch around. (yes, we somehow managed to get maths involved) Always chop from the top, not from the side.

Voila!

Tips and Tricks

The magic behind

If you hate cutting onions owing to all the crying involved, we have you sorted. If you wear contact lenses simply make sure to have them on. That's it - you are now immuned. Wearing googles which keep the air out also helps - swimming googles would do. Glasses won't. If you try the googles idea, please feel free to share your pictures with us.

Put your onion in a blender - no crying involved - the machine protects you and it would do the job faster than you. Unfortunately there is the additional cleaning of the blender involved but you can't have it all.

Tips and Tricks

The magic behind

Roasting the nuts would speed up the blending process. However this does give the butter a certain flavour, which some love and some don't - it is all in the taste buds.

The coconut flavour combines really nicely with butters such as Almond and Peanut, experiment with unsweetened shredded coconut - simply add it to the nuts in the blender.

Use spices or natural flavouring to make your butter more exciting. Cinnamon and vanilla are classic choices and you would be surpirised at the blend of flavours and aroma they create.

Play with mixing nuts (almonds, peanuts, cashews, walnuts...), you can vary your proportions and choose how many to combine.

Ingredients

  • 150g Honey
  • 150g Coconut Oil
  • 200g Oat Flour
  • 200g Whole Wheat Flour
  • 150g Almonds
  • 1tsp Baking Powder
  • 3tsp Vanilla Powder
  • 2tsp Cinnamon
  • 200g Melted Milk Chocolate

Instructions

Preheat your over to 165 degrees (fan).

Melt the honey and coconut oil.

Mix the Oat Flour, Whole Wheat Flour, Baking Powder, Vanilla and Cinnamon.

Add the honey and oil to the dry Mix.

Melt the milk chocolate, add to the mix.

Chop the almonds into small pieces, add to the mix.

Form small balls from the dough with your hand, after you arrange them on the baking tray flatten them (you can use a wooden spoon).

Cook for 10 minutes.
